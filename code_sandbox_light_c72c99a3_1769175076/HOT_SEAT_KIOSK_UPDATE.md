## Game Mechanics (Clarified)

### **How The Hot Seat Works**
1. **Guest sits in the hot seat**
2. **Kiosk displays rapid-fire questions** (automated)
3. **Guest answers quickly** (video recorded)
4. **Questions appear on screen** for everyone to see
5. **Hilarious moments captured** for the wedding video archive

### **Benefits of Kiosk-Generated Questions**
- ✅ Pre-screened, wedding-appropriate content
- ✅ Perfect timing and pacing
- ✅ No awkward moments from unprepared guests
- ✅ Professional quality every time
- ✅ Guests can play alone or with friends watching
