Questions & Answers

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to the most common questions about Bold Booth

How much space does Bold Booth need?

The booth itself requires approximately 6ft x 6ft of space. We also recommend having access to a wall or open area within 20 feet for the projector display. Our team will work with your venue to find the perfect spot!

What if my venue has bad WiFi?

No problem! Bold Booth runs on a completely private network‚Äîwe don't rely on venue WiFi at all. This means zero technical issues, no buffering, and 100% uptime guaranteed.

When do we get our videos?

All videos are delivered on a USB drive within 1 week (or less!) after your event. The USB includes every video, organized by game and timestamp, plus a highlight reel we create for you.

Can we customize the games and questions?

Absolutely! Through your client portal, you can write custom trivia questions, upload personal photos, create your own prompts, and even disable games you don't want. We encourage customization!

What happens if there's a technical issue?

Our professional attendant is on-site for your entire event to handle any technical issues immediately. That said, Bold Booth's private network architecture means technical problems are extremely rare. We have a 100% uptime track record!

Is there a guest limit?

Nope! Bold Booth works for intimate gatherings of 30 guests or grand celebrations of 300+. The games scale beautifully, and the competitive leaderboard actually gets MORE exciting with larger groups!

Do you provide a projector and screen?

We can connect to your venue's existing projector/screen setup, or we can provide our own display equipment for an additional fee. During your consultation, we'll discuss what works best.

What's included in the 4-hour package?

Everything! 10+ interactive games, professional attendant, complete setup and breakdown, private network technology, HD video recording, USB delivery within one week, pre-event customization call, and optional guest viewing page.

Can guests see their videos immediately?

Yes! Guests can watch their gameplay and reactions on the booth screen right after recording. Plus, selected videos rotate on the big projector display throughout your reception.

Is Bold Booth appropriate for all ages?

Absolutely! The games are designed to be fun for everyone‚Äîfrom kids to grandparents. The touchscreen interface is intuitive enough for any age, and you can customize content to match your guest demographic.

What makes Bold Booth different from a regular photo booth?

Traditional photo booths give you static images. Bold Booth captures dynamic video, interactive gameplay, authentic reactions, and heartfelt messages‚Äîall while entertaining your ENTIRE reception via the big screen.

Do you travel outside your local area?

Yes! We service events throughout the region and can travel to destination celebrations. Travel fees may apply for events outside our primary service area. Contact us with your venue location for a custom quote!
