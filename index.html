Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bold Booth

How much space does Bold Booth need?

+

The kiosk itself needs approximately 6 feet x 6 feet of floor space. We also need space for the projector display area (ideally within 20 feet of the kiosk for optimal cable management). We'll work with your venue layout to find the perfect spot!

Do you need WiFi from our venue?

+

Nope! Bold Booth runs on a private, secure local network that we bring with us. This means zero dependency on your venue's WiFi and guaranteed 100% uptime. No dropped connections, no slow speeds, no issues‚Äîever.

When do we get our videos?

+

Within one week or less, you'll receive a beautiful, branded USB drive containing every single video message, game reaction, and candid moment captured throughout your event. Plus, we can create an optional online viewing page where all your wedding guests can watch and relive the memories!

Can we customize the games and questions?

+

Absolutely! After booking, you'll get access to our client portal where you can write custom trivia questions, upload personal photos, create your own Truth or Dare prompts, and personalize every detail. You can also enable/disable any game to match your event vibe.

What if something goes wrong during our event?

+

We provide a professional on-site attendant for the duration of your event who monitors everything and troubleshoots instantly. Our private network means no connectivity issues, and we've engineered Bold Booth for 100% uptime. In 150+ events, we've never had a single failure.

How many guests can use Bold Booth?

+

Bold Booth works perfectly for intimate gatherings of 30 guests or large celebrations of 300+. The kiosk handles continuous use throughout your event, and the leaderboard/team competition keeps everyone engaged regardless of guest count.

Can we use our venue's projector/screen?

+

Yes! If your venue has a projector or large screen, we can connect to it. If not, we can provide our own projector and screen setup for an additional fee. Either way, you'll get the full big-screen experience.

What's included in the 4-hour package?

+

Everything! 10+ interactive games, professional on-site attendant, complete setup and breakdown, private network technology, HD video recording, USB drive delivery within 1 week, pre-event customization call, and optional online guest viewing page. You don't need to worry about a thing.

Can guests see their videos immediately?

+

Yes! After recording, guests can see their gameplay and reactions on the kiosk screen. Plus, selected videos and highlights rotate on the big projector display throughout the event, so everyone can enjoy the best moments in real-time.

Is Bold Booth appropriate for all ages?

+

Absolutely! We design games to be inclusive and family-friendly. Kids love the emoji puzzles and quick messages, while adults enjoy the trivia and group voting games. You can also customize content to match your audience (G-rated for family events or slightly edgier for adult-only celebrations).

How is Bold Booth different from a regular photo booth?

+

Traditional photo booths capture still photos in a closed box. Bold Booth is a fully interactive entertainment experience‚Äîdynamic video games, live competition, big-screen display, customizable content, and real-time engagement. It's not just documentation; it's entertainment that becomes a time capsule.
